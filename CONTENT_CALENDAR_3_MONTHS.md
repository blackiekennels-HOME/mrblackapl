## CAPTION WRITING TIPS

### 1. Hook First (First 2 lines)
- Ask a question
- Make a bold statement
- Share an emotional moment
- Use a statistic

### 2. Tell a Story (Middle section)
- Describe the situation
- Show the transformation
- Highlight the impact
- Build emotional connection

### 3. Clear CTA (Last 2 lines)
- Direct action (Apply, Donate, Learn More)
- Link in bio
- Specific next step

### 4. Emoji Strategy
- Use 3-5 emojis per caption
- Place strategically (beginning, middle, end)
- Match emoji to content
- Don't overuse

### 5. Formatting
- Break into short paragraphs
- Use line breaks for readability
- Bold key phrases (use **)
- Use emojis as section dividers
