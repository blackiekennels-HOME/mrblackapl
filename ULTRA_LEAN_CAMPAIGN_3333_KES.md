## Optimization Strategy by Month

### Month 1: Foundation & Testing
- **Focus:** Establish baseline performance, test creative
- **Action:** Run all campaigns at equal budget
- **Optimization:** Pause underperforming ad sets after 7 days
- **Expected:** Identify top-performing platform

### Month 2: Optimization & Scaling
- **Focus:** Double down on winners, refine targeting
- **Action:** Reallocate 10% budget from underperformer to top performer
- **Optimization:** A/B test new creative on winning platform
- **Expected:** Reduce cost per volunteer by 20-30%

### Month 3: Scaling & Maximization
- **Focus:** Scale winning platform, prepare for post-campaign
- **Action:** Reallocate additional 10% budget to top performer
- **Optimization:** Launch referral program for organic growth
- **Expected:** Reduce cost per volunteer by additional 15-20%

## Conclusion

With a lean 3,333 KES monthly budget (10,000 KES total over 3 months), Mr. Black APL can realistically recruit **36-60 active volunteers** with a cost per volunteer of **KES 222-555**. Success depends on:

1. **Organic content excellence** (70% of strategy) - Free reach through authentic storytelling
2. **Precision targeting** (20% of strategy) - Narrow audience selection for lower CPC
3. **Paid amplification** (10% of strategy) - Boost top-performing organic content

**Key Success Factor:** TikTok's algorithm-driven reach offers the best ROI. By focusing 40% of budget on paid promotion of viral organic videos, Mr. Black APL can achieve exponential follower growth (700%+) and recruit volunteers at the lowest cost per volunteer (KES 22-123).

**Timeline:** Month 1 establishes baseline, Month 2 optimizes, Month 3 scales. Post-campaign, expand budget or consolidate to highest-ROI platform based on performance.
