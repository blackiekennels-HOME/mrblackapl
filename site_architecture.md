# Mr. Black APL Website Architecture & Content Plan

## 1. Core Architecture & Navigation
- **Primary Navigation**: Home | About | Rescue | Rehabilitation | Adoption | Foster | Volunteer Hub | Events | Blog | Contact
- **Secondary Navigation (Top/Footer)**: Donate | Patron | Mr. Black Centers
- **Floating Element**: Sitewide WhatsApp Chat Button (0794 277 833)

## 2. Page Structures

### Home Page
- **Hero**: Headline "Rescue • Rehabilitate • Rehome", Subheadline "Empowering Compassion Across Kenya". CTAs: Report Now, Donate Now, Join Rescue, Chat WhatsApp.
- **Blackie's Story**: Emotional section about Blackie's legacy.
- **Mission & Stats**: Overview of impact.
- **Featured Pets**: Carousel of adoptable animals.
- **Programs Overview**: Brief intro to Rescue, Rehab, Rehome.
- **Get Involved**: Volunteer and donation appeals.
- **Social Proof**: Testimonials and Instagram feed.

### About Page
- **Our Story**: The journey of Mr. Black APL and Blackie's legacy.
- **Mission, Vision, Values**: Core organizational pillars.
- **Why It Matters**: The state of animal protection in Kenya.
- **Future Expansion**: Plans for nationwide centers.
- **CTA**: Support Mission.

### Rescue Page
- **Emergency Reporting**: Clear instructions and form link (Report Now).
- **Volunteer Roles**: Join Rescue, Offer Transport, Lead Rescue, Scout Areas, Assist Rescue.
- **Operations**: How the rescue process works.

### Rehabilitation Page
- **Medical & Behavioral Care**: Overview of veterinary support, recovery, and training.
- **Volunteer Roles**: Join Vets, Lead Rehab, Train Dogs, Build With Us, Become Foster, Support Rehab, Plan Programs.

### Adoption Page
- **Available Pets**: Gallery of animals looking for homes.
- **Process & Requirements**: How to adopt.
- **Success Stories**: Happy tales.
- **Volunteer Roles**: Adopt Today, Coordinate Adoptions, Screen Adopters, Create Listings, Follow Up.

### Foster Page
- **Foster Program**: Temporary boarding and daycare details.
- **CTA**: Become Foster.

### Volunteer Hub
- **Central Directory**: Links to all 28 operational and volunteer forms.
- **Team Groups**: Links to WhatsApp onboarding flows.
- **CTA**: Join APL.

### Donation & Patron Pages
- **Donation**: Impact-based giving (KES 100, 500, 1000). CTA: Donate Now.
- **Patron**: Monthly tiers (Paw Friend, Caregiver, Guardian, Champion). CTA: Become Patron.

### Additional Pages
- **Events**: Fundraisers, adoption drives, outreach.
- **Blog**: SEO-focused categories (Rescue Stories, Animal Welfare Kenya, etc.).
- **Contact**: WhatsApp, Email, Socials, Maps.
- **Mr. Black Centers**: Expansion plans, land volunteering (Offer Land).

## 3. SEO Strategy
- **Keywords**: dog adoption Kenya, animal rescue Kenya, adopt dogs Nairobi, rescue abandoned dog Kenya, foster dogs Kenya, volunteer animal shelter Kenya, donate to animal rescue Kenya, pet adoption Kenya, dog trainers Kenya, veterinary volunteers Kenya, foster abandoned pets Kenya.
- **Technical**: Schema markup (Organization, FAQ, Event, Donation), XML sitemap, Image alt tags, Meta titles/descriptions.

## 4. Design Guidelines
- **Colors**: Black background, White text, Gold/Orange accents.
- **Typography**: Clean, legible sans-serif.
- **Tone**: Professional, Emotional, Urgent, Trustworthy.
