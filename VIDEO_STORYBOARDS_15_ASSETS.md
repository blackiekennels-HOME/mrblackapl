### Best for: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok (High engagement)

### VIDEO 1: "RESCUE IN PROGRESS" (30 seconds)
**Platform Priority:** TikTok, Instagram Reels
**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Trending Sound:** Use trending rescue/action sound (search "rescue" or "action" on TikTok)

#### Storyboard

| Time | Shot | Description | Text Overlay | Music/Sound |
|------|------|-------------|--------------|------------|
| 0-3s | Wide Shot | Rescue team arriving at location (street, park) | "RESCUE IN PROGRESS" (Bold, Yellow) | Trending action sound (intro) |
| 3-8s | Close-up | Volunteer carefully approaching injured animal | "Every second counts" (White, Center) | Action sound continues |
| 8-15s | Medium Shot | Volunteer gently handling animal, checking condition | "We're here to help" (White, Center) | Action sound + soft background music |
| 15-22s | Close-up | Animal's face, showing relief/trust | "Trust is everything" (Yellow, Bottom) | Emotional music swell |
| 22-30s | Wide Shot | Rescue team transporting animal to vehicle, sunset/golden hour background | "Mr. Black APL - Join our rescue team" (Yellow, Bottom) + CTA button | Uplifting music, trending sound outro |

#### Production Notes
- **Best Time to Film:** Golden hour (sunset) for emotional lighting
- **Camera Angle:** Mix of wide shots (context) and close-ups (emotion)
- **Text Style:** Bold, sans-serif font (Montserrat or Poppins in CapCut)
- **Color Scheme:** Yellow text on dark background for contrast
- **CTA:** "Join Rescue Team" button linking to form
- **Music Recommendation:** "Inspiring Journey" (YouTube Audio Library) or trending TikTok rescue sound

#### CapCut Production Steps
1. Import 3-4 short video clips of rescue operation
2. Trim each clip to fit timing above
3. Add text overlays at specified times with fade-in/fade-out transitions
4. Add trending sound from TikTok audio library
5. Add background music (YouTube Audio Library - "Inspiring Journey")
6. Apply color correction (increase saturation, warm tones)
7. Add CTA button/text at end
8. Export as vertical 9:16 format

### Week 1: Pre-Production
- **Day 1-2:** Gather all photos and video clips from your organization
- **Day 3-4:** Organize clips by category (rescue, volunteers, animals, care)
- **Day 5-7:** Create storyboards and plan filming schedule

- **Film volunteer day-in-life** (Video 4)

- **Videos 1-3:** Rescue operation videos (3 days)

- **Videos 7-9:** Animal success stories (3 days)
- **Videos 10-12:** Call-to-action videos (2 days)

- **Review all videos** for quality and messaging

### Week 7: Launch & Distribution
- **Upload to TikTok** (Videos 1, 3, 5, 10, 13, 14, 15)
