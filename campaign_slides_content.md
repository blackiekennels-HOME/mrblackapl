# Slide 16: Risk Mitigation Strategy
## Addressing Potential Challenges

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Low submission rate** | Campaign fails | Test creatives early, optimize underperformers |
| **High drop-off after submission** | Wasted budget | 24-hour follow-up, clear onboarding |
| **Volunteer burnout** | Retention issues | Flexible scheduling, recognition program |
| **Ad fatigue** | Declining CTR | Rotate creatives every 2 weeks |
| **Algorithm changes** | Reduced reach | Maintain organic content buffer |

**Contingency Plan:**
- Weekly performance reviews
- Quick pivot to underperforming platforms
- Budget reallocation based on data
